How DigiMaat works

We check. We explain. We help.

Here is exactly what DigiMaat does — and what happens if we find something.

What we check
Data leaks
We check if your email address has been stolen in a hack of websites like LinkedIn or Facebook.
Suspicious messages
We look for fake messages pretending to be your bank, the tax office, DigiD, or PostNL — and dangerous links.
Your inbox
With your permission, we scan your email inbox and automatically flag anything suspicious.
If we find a risk
1
We show you exactly what was found — in plain language, no technical jargon.
2
We give you simple step-by-step instructions: delete, block, or change your password.
3
With one tap you can ask your family member for help — they receive a message explaining what happened.
4
We confirm when the danger has been handled — so you can feel safe again.
🔒 Your messages are never stored. We only show you the result — nothing is saved on our servers.
